Şahsenem Saphira
Şahsenem Saphira( born 1st April 2000) is a German and Turkish singer and songwriter. She possesses a Mezzo-Soprano vocal range and is known for her ambiguous melancholic pop sound. After releasing her second single, "Flaws", she was discovered and instantly gained recognition for her soothing and seductive vocals.

Featured on the cover of "Eighteen Magazine" in 2022.

Saphira's Multilingual Musical Tapestry

Diving into a multilingual realm, Saphira crafts enchanting melodies in the harmonious tongues of English, German, and Turkish. Her music becomes a vivid tapestry, celebrating the intricate beauty of these languages while delving into the rich tapestry of cultural diversity forged through her life's journey.
